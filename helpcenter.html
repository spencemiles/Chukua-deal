Welcome to Chukua Deal

What is Chukua Deal?

Chukua Deal is your trusted online marketplace for buying and selling second-hand products. We connect real buyers and sellers in a safe, fast, and easy way. Whether you're looking for electronics, fashion, furniture, vehicles, property, or tools, Chukua Deal gives you access to affordable and verified listings from real people around you.

How does Chukua Deal work?

Buyers browse listings and contact sellers directly. Both parties agree on price and delivery. The transaction is completed between buyer and seller. We act as a bridge to make the process flexible and full of opportunities.

How to Buy Items

Steps to buy an item
  1. Search for the item you want
  2. Open the product listing
  3. Check description, images, and price
  4. Click "Contact Seller"
  5. Negotiate price if needed
  6. Agree on meeting or delivery method

We recommend meeting sellers in safe public places when possible.

Trust & Safety Guidelines

Safety Tips for Buyers & Sellers
  • Always verify the item before paying
  • Avoid sending money before seeing proof
  • Meet in safe public places
  • Use trusted payment methods like M-Pesa
  • Report suspicious users immediately
Prohibited Items

No fake listings allowed. No illegal products. No harassment or spam. Users must respect each other.

Payments

How do payments work?

Chukua Deal encourages safe transactions between users. We do not hold payments directly, so users must be careful when dealing with sellers or buyers. We recommend using M-Pesa or meeting in person for cash transactions.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Chukua Deal free?
Yes, browsing is completely free! Selling features will be announced soon.
Do you deliver products?
No, buyers and sellers arrange delivery themselves.
Can I sell used items?
Yes, once the selling feature launches, all second-hand items in good condition will be welcome.
How do I create an account?
