About Chukua Deal

Kenya's most trusted marketplace for quality second-hand items

Our Story

Chukua Deal was born from a simple idea: make buying and selling second-hand items easy, safe, and accessible to everyone in Kenya. We saw a gap in the market where people struggled to find quality pre-owned items at fair prices, while sellers had nowhere reliable to list their products.

Starting in 2022, we built Chukua Deal to connect buyers and sellers in a transparent, trustworthy environment. Today, we're proud to be one of Kenya's fastest-growing second-hand marketplaces, with thousands of successful transactions and happy customers across the country.

Our journey is just beginning, and we're committed to continuously improving our platform to serve you better.

Our Mission

To empower Kenyans to buy and sell quality second-hand items easily, affordably, and safely, while promoting sustainable consumption and reducing waste.

Our Vision

To become Kenya's most trusted and go-to marketplace for second-hand goods, creating a circular economy that benefits both people and the planet.

Our Core Values
